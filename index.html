About Kelivah

Kelivah is the stage name of Kelvin Ichokwu, an Afro-fusion artist from Delta State, Nigeria. His music focuses on emotional storytelling, melody-driven Afro-influenced sound, and real life experience expression. Kelivah works as an independent digital music creator, distributing songs through global streaming platforms while building long-term artistic presence.
