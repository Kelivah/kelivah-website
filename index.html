About

Kelivah is an Afro-fusion artist blending emotion, melody, and real-life experiences into honest, reflective sound. His music explores growth, patience, imagination, and everyday truth, shaped by rhythm and sincerity.

Stage Name: Kelivah
Real Name: Kelvin Ichokwu
Origin: Delta State, Nigeria

Kelivah began his music journey in mid-2024, creating beats and recording with mobile apps. In February 2026, he officially released his first single "Green Light". That same month, three tracks were released: "Green Light", "Dreams", and "Happy Birthday (Great Day)".

Currently a 300-level Biochemistry student at the Federal University of Technology, Minna, balancing academics and artistry.
